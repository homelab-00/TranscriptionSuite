TranscriptionSuite — Apple Silicon (Metal) Installation Instructions
======================================================================

1. Copy TranscriptionSuite.app to your Applications folder
   • Drag TranscriptionSuite.app from this DMG window into the Applications
     folder shortcut on the right.

2. Open the app
   • Double-click TranscriptionSuite in your Applications folder, or launch
     it from Spotlight / Launchpad / your favourite app launcher.

3. If you see an error when opening the app
   ─────────────────────────────────────────
   macOS may show a message such as:

     "TranscriptionSuite" Not Opened. Apple could not verify
     "TranscriptionSuite" is free of malware that may harm your Mac
     or compromise your privacy.

   or:

     "TranscriptionSuite" is damaged and can't be opened.

   This happens because the app is not signed with an Apple Developer
   certificate.  You can allow it using either method below:

   Option A — Terminal (one command)
   ~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
   Open Terminal (Applications → Utilities → Terminal) and run:

     xattr -dr com.apple.quarantine /Applications/TranscriptionSuite.app

   Then try opening the app again.

   Option B — System Settings
   ~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
   1. Open System Settings → Privacy & Security.
   2. Scroll to the Security section at the bottom.
   3. Next to the message "'TranscriptionSuite' was blocked to protect
      your Mac", click "Open Anyway".
   4. Authenticate with your password or Touch ID when prompted.
